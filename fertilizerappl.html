Organic Fertilizer Application

Interdisciplinary Design Projects / Winter-Spring 2020

Teammates:

Claudia Gabison
Matt Zients

Skills:

Interdisciplinary Design

As part of my design capstone, I am working in a team of students with an organic farm co-op of 80+ rice farmers in Thailand to help develop a custom fertilizer applicator for a UV-sensitive, aerobic fertilizer they are using. We are currently working on a prototype that can be attached to a tractor for efficiency and ease of use.
