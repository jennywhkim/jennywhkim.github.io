HairTrap: A Hair Cleaner for Showers

Human-Centered Product Design / Winter 2019

Teammates:
Alexis Chen
Natalie Hanson
Emily Karl
Sydney Stelzer

Skills:
Iterative Prototyping
3D Printing
Laser Cutting
Design of Experiments

For one of my Segal Design Certificate classes, my teammates and I worked on a white-space project. We explored the difficulty in long, tangled hair clinging to walls and drains in showers. After research, brainstorming, and an iterative brainstorming process, we developed a brush that would help collect tangled hair and dispose of it quickly in an environmentally friendly way without having to touch it.
