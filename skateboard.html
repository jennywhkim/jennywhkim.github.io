For my mechanical engineering capstone, I'm working in a team of undergraduate and graduate students to design and build a robot that can perform skateboarding tricks on a half-pipe. I have been working on the mechanical design and the power-level systems of the robot. Currently, we are finalizing the design of the robot and entering the building phase. This has been a great way to wrap up my undergrad courses in engineering in mechanical analysis, manufacturing, and mechatronics.


Background

We were tasked with designing and building a 2-DOF robot that could perform three skateboarding tricks: drop-in, stall, and manual.
